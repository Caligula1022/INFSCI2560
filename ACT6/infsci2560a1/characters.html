Name: David Martinez

A Hispanic teenager who was a top student at the prestigious Arasaka Academy. Due to being born to a poor family, he is constantly bullied by his classmates and feels like he doesn't belong. A sudden and devastating personal tragedy lead him to abandon his education and puts him on the path of becoming an edgerunner.

Name: Lucyna Kushinada

A young netrunner who becomes romantically involved with David and introduces him to the criminal underworld of Night City. She has a particular hatred towards Arasaka and dreams of traveling to the Moon.
